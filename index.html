IDEATHON

You all might have faced different problems in Mumbai but have you ever thought of taking part in developing Mumbai. Here's a golden chance to showcase your creative ideas.

Ideathons is a research, brainstorming and sprint event. The team will have to throw in their ideas, present them to get feedback and what they should be working on or how they should go about it.


During Ideathon you along with your team come together to brainstorm on a certain issue and provide a solution to it using analytical research, market analysis, theoretical and practical knowledge. The implementable ideas have to be presented with a proof of concept and a research paper has to be prepared in the end.
